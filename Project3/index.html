In "Alice in Wonderland" young Alice follows a white rabbit down a rabbit hole and finds herself in a peculiar and enchanting world called Wonderland. Throughout her journey, she encounters a series of eccentric and whimsical characters, such as the Cheshire Cat, the Mad Hatter, and the Queen of Hearts. Alice navigates through a topsy-turvy realm where logic is often turned upside down, and nothing is as it seems.
