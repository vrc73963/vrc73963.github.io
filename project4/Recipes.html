Quick Recipes

1. The Novel Twist

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 0.5 oz Elderflower liqueur
  • Fresh cucumber slices
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Top with tonic water

Instructions:

Muddle cucumber and mint in a glass. Add gin and elderflower liqueur, then fill with ice. Top with tonic water and garnish with a cucumber slice and mint sprig.

2. The Literary Sour

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 0.75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz Raspberry syrup
  • Egg white (optional)
  • Fresh raspberries for garnish

Instructions:

Shake bourbon, lemon juice, raspberry syrup, and egg white (if using) with ice. Strain into a glass and garnish with fresh raspberries.
